This is my corner of the internet — a place to write down what I'm learning and
share my thoughts as they form.

## Why write?

Writing forces clarity. When I try to explain an idea, I quickly find the gaps in
my own understanding. So this blog is partly selfish: it's how I think.

## What to expect

- **Reviews** of the books, papers, and blogs I read.
- **Posts** on technology, finance, and personal growth.

Thanks for reading. To add your own post, drop a Markdown file into
`src/content/blog/`.
