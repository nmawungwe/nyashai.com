## Summary

Newport argues that the ability to focus without distraction on a cognitively
demanding task is becoming both rare and valuable — and that you can train it.

## My take

The framing is sharp and the first half is excellent. Some of the tactics felt
familiar, but the core argument changed how I structure my mornings.
